Welcome to Cyber Trail, a website about my journey through web design, technology, and cybersecurity. Instead of putting too much personal information online, this site focuses on my learning path, goals, and interest in building safer digital spaces.

This class helped me practice HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images, videos, forms, tables, links, and page layouts. At first, building webpages felt like trying to read a secret hacker scroll, but after working through the assignments, the pieces started making sense.

My main interest is cybersecurity, and web design connects to that more than people might think. If you understand how websites are built, you can better understand how they break, how attackers target them, and how to protect them.
