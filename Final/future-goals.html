My Future Goals

My future goals are connected to technology, cybersecurity, and continuing to build practical skills. I want to keep learning how networks, systems, websites, and security tools work so I can become better prepared for my next career in IT.

Web design is useful because it teaches structure, problem-solving, and attention to detail. Those same skills matter in cybersecurity. A missing tag in HTML may break a layout, but a missing security step can create a much bigger problem.

After finishing my degree, I will continue to work in a technology role where I help protect systems, troubleshoot problems, and keep improving. My goal is to become the kind of IT person who can fix the issue, explain the issue, and not just say, “Have you tried turning it off and back on?” even though sometimes that really does work.
